Types of Data types(Primitive) 
1. ** Number ** : Both decimal and integer value.
2. ** String ** : Sequence of characters.
3. ** Boolean ** : True or false.
4. ** Undefined ** : empty value 
For ex:- `let children` .
5. ** Null ** 
6. ** Symbol ** (ES2015) : Unique and cannot be changed
7. ** BigInt ** (ES2020) : Large Integers than the Number type can hold.
